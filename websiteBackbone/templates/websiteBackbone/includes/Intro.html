History - From the Founder

This company started as a Django based blog for me to document my various technological endeavors.

Since I was a kid, I was always interested in computers. my first virus and my father taught me how to reload an OS. Eventually, I started developing for a FIRST Robotics team in high school with a PHP back end and HTML/CSS front end. I built my first computer shortly after that. It was a simple start and I've continually added onto my skills with time. A lot of opportunities have come my way but throughout college I ended up developing more and did some basic IT support for three years. I graduated college as a Biomedical Engineer and followed up shortly after as a junior production engineer learning the software development life cycle and operations in a professional setting.

I wanted to utilize everything I was learning and to learn more! I established a homelab where I could maintain my own code and infrastructure. I had the servers to run, but I didn't have any exciting code to present to the world. I played around with the infrastructure, but eventually, this needed to make it into production. It took a month while I worked and before I knew it, I had pierced the heavens and was live in the Cloud.
